Privacy Policy for GuitarPal

Effective date: January 30, 2026

GuitarPal ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This app stores all data locally on your device and does not collect or transmit any personal or device information.

1. Data Access

  • Microphone: Accessed only when the user records riffs in-app. Audio is stored locally and never shared.
  • Local storage: Used to save recordings, practice history, song lists, and app settings. All data remains on the device.

2. Third-Party Services

GuitarPal does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

3. Children's Privacy

GuitarPal is not intended for children under 13. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no information about children stored or shared.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised "Effective date".
